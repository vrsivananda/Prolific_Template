Task:
If you elect to participate in this study, you will be asked to do the following task: Respond to two simple survey questions.

The entire study should last about 1 minute. It is important that you complete the study while in a quiet place where you can focus without distraction. Please be sure that you have enough time and are in an appropriate setting before continuing with the study.

As with any task requiring that you focus intently on the computer monitor, you may experience some eye fatigue or dryness. Feel free to take a break whenever you need to.

Eligibility:
In order to participate, you must be using a computer (this should not be done on a mobile device or iPad), be using Google Chrome as your web browser (Safari, Firefox, Internet Explorer, etc. will not work), and must have normal or corrected-to-normal vision (glasses or contacts are OK! Just make sure you are wearing them when you participate in this study). Please DO NOT participate if you have a history of seizures, epilepsy, or stroke, as the stimuli we present in the experiment will be briefly flashed, and this is a potential (but unlikely) seizure trigger. Finally, if you have previously participated in an experiment about judging emotions that looks similar to this one, we would appreciate it if you abstained from participating, as we would like to evaluate participants that have never done this task before.
