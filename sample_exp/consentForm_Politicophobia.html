Study:
If you elect to participate in this study, you will be asked to do the following task: answer a demographic questionnaire, a scale on politicophobia and a number of psychological scales. This should take approximately 20 to 60 minutes.

It is important that you complete the study while in a quiet place where you can focus without distraction. Please be sure that you have enough time and are in an appropriate setting beforehand.

As with any task requiring that you focus intently on the computer monitor, you may experience some eye fatigue or dryness. Feel free to take a break whenever you need to.

Eligibility:
In order to participate, you must be using a computer (this should not be done on a mobile device or iPad), be using Google Chrome as your web browser (Safari, Firefox, Internet Explorer, etc. will not work), be 18 years or older and either a citizen of the United States or a permanent resident of Hong Kong.

Compensation:
You will be compensated $2 for completion of the study.

You may choose to withdraw from the survey at any point, and we also reserve the right to terminate your session at any time. Note, however, that if you withdraw, or if your session is terminated, you will not be compensated. Additionally, you will need to email uclaqualialab@gmail.com, notifying the surveyors of when you participated (please include day and time in the email), so you can be manually compensated through the mTurk system.

You will not directly benefit from your participation in the research study, other than contributing to psychological research.

Privacy and Confidentiality:
Any information that is obtained in connection with this study and that can identify you will remain confidential. It will be disclosed only with your permission or as required by law. Any work performed on mTurk can be linked to your profile page. You may therefore wish to restrict what information you share in your public profile. If you choose to contact us directly either at the email address below or through mTurk, your email name may be seen. mTurk worker IDs will not be shared with anyone. mTurk worker IDs are collected solely for the purposes of distributing compensation and will not be connected with the data or shared with anyone outside of the research team.
